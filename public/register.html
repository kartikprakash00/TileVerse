How to Play!

  1. Use your arrow keys or to move the tiles up or down and or to move the tiles left or right.
  2. When two tiles with the same number touch, they merge into one.
  3. After merging each tile, you gain points and your score will increase.
  4. The more tiles you merge, the higher your score will get.
